ANIMALS MAP PLACES

AMPHITHEATRE

The amphitheatre is a large, open-air venue used for entertainment, performances, and events. It is designed to provide excellent acoustics and visibility for audiences.

INSECT HOUSE

The insect house is a specialized habitat designed to showcase various insect species. It provides a controlled environment for insects to thrive and allows visitors to observe their behavior up close.

LOST FOREST

The lost forest is a hidden gem within the zoo, featuring a diverse range of plant and animal species. It provides a unique opportunity for visitors to explore a lush, tropical environment.

MONKEY TRAIL

The monkey trail is an interactive exhibit that allows visitors to observe various monkey species in a naturalistic setting. It features climbing structures and lush vegetation to mimic the monkeys' natural habitat.

PIZZA SHOP

The pizza shop offers a variety of delicious pizzas and snacks for visitors to enjoy during their time at the zoo. It provides a family-friendly dining experience with outdoor seating options.

RESTAURANT

The restaurant offers a diverse menu featuring a variety of cuisines, providing a comfortable dining experience for visitors. It is an ideal place to relax and enjoy a meal during your visit to the zoo.
