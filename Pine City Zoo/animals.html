ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.

GEMSBOKS

Gemsboks are large antelopes known for their striking appearance and ability to survive in arid environments.

GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and unique coat patterns.

GORILLAS

Gorillas are the largest primates, known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.

KOALAS

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their tree-dwelling habits and diet of eucalyptus leaves.

LIONS

Lions are social big cats known for their majestic manes and powerful roars.

MONKEYS

Monkeys are intelligent primates known for their playful behavior and social structures.

PANDAS

Pandas are large bears native to China, known for their distinctive black and white coloring and diet of bamboo.

TIGERS

Tigers are the largest cat species, known for their powerful build and striking orange coat with black stripes.

WARTHOGS

Warthogs are wild pigs known for their distinctive tusks and rugged appearance, often found in African savannas.
